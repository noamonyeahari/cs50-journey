How I would write the algorithm for selling a plot of land;

START

Receive Inquiry

Via phone, email, walk-in, referral, or online platform


Greet & Qualify the Client

Collect full name, contact details, and ID
Determine: buyer, investor, or developer?
Assess budget range and financing status (cash or mortgage)


Understand Client Requirements

Preferred location / area
Plot size (sqm or acres or hectares)
Intended use (investment, residential, commercial, agricultural)
Timeline to purchase
Any specific preferences (corner plot, road frontage, etc.)


Search & Match Available Listings

Query portfolio/database for matching plots
Filter by location, size, price, and zoning


Present Available Properties

Share listing details: location, size, price, title type, photos/maps
Highlight key selling points for each option
Provide comparison if multiple options exist


Schedule Site Inspection

Arrange visit at client's convenience
Accompany client to site
Point out boundaries, access roads, utilities, and neighborhood features


Address Questions & Objections

Clarify title documents, zoning laws, and land use
Handle price objections with comparable market data
Reassure on legal process and security of transaction


Client Decision Point

✅ Interested → Proceed to Step 9
❌ Not Interested → Return to Step 4 or log feedback and follow up later


Initiate Offer & Negotiation

Receive verbal or written offer from client
Present offer to landowner / management
Negotiate price, payment terms, and conditions
Reach mutually agreed terms


Issue Letter of Offer / Proposal

Document agreed price, payment plan, and conditions
Client reviews and signs to confirm intent to purchase


Collect Initial Deposit / Commitment Fee

Issue official receipt
Property is reserved/taken off market


Due Diligence & Documentation

Verify title with land registry
Confirm no encumbrances, disputes, or government acquisition
Engage lawyer to review or prepare Contract of Sale


Sign Sale Agreement / Contract

Both parties execute the contract
Lawyer witnesses and retains copies


Balance Payment

Client pays outstanding balance per agreed schedule
Confirm receipt and issue final payment acknowledgment


Process Title Transfer

Prepare Deed of Assignment or Conveyance
Pay applicable fees: stamp duty, consent fee, capital gains tax
File documents at the Land Registry


Hand Over Title Documents

Deliver original documents to client
Provide copies of all transaction receipts and correspondence


Physical Handover of Land

Walk the client around the plot
Confirm boundary beacons/markers are intact
Hand over any access keys, gate codes, or physical materials


Post-Sale Follow-Up

Check client satisfaction
Offer additional services (development, fencing, construction referrals)
Request referrals or testimonials
Update CRM/database with completed sale record


Close File

Archive all transaction documents
Record commission and close the deal file




END


Key Rule Throughout: At every stage, I will maintain transparency with the client, protect both parties legally, and never transfer title before full payment or secured financing is confirmed.
